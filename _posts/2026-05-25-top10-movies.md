## How I Chose These Films

Before the list, I want to briefly explain how I chose these films.

This is not an objective ranking. My taste is shaped by my personal experiences and biases. However, I still tried to judge these films beyond personal attachment, considering their story, visuals, performances, atmosphere, and the lasting impression they left on me.

I also chose only one film from each director. Even if I love several works by the same directors, I would rather use one film as a representative.
